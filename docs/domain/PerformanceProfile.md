# 1. Purpose

The purpose of the `PerformanceProfile` entity is to represent the overall temporal behaviour of the analysed ensemble throughout an entire musical performance.

It provides a unified analytical description derived from the interaction of all InstrumentProfile entities.

---

# 2. Scientific Motivation

The rhythmic behaviour of an ensemble cannot be completely understood by analysing individual performers independently.

Musical interaction emerges collectively.

The PerformanceProfile integrates the analytical information extracted from every SoundSource into a coherent description of the entire performance.

---

# 3. Scientific Definition

A `PerformanceProfile` is an analytical entity describing the temporal characteristics of a complete musical performance.

It is derived from the Internal Metric Timeline together with the InstrumentProfiles generated during the analysis.

The PerformanceProfile represents analytical knowledge rather than observable musical events.

---

---

# 5. Responsibilities

The PerformanceProfile is responsible for:

- representing the overall temporal behaviour of the ensemble;
- integrating all InstrumentProfiles;
- providing a complete analytical description of the analysed performance;
- supporting comparative analysis between performances.

The PerformanceProfile never:

- modifies the Internal Metric Timeline;
- estimates pulse;
- identifies musical events.

---

# 6. Relationships

A PerformanceProfile is composed of one or more InstrumentProfiles.

It is derived from:

- InternalMetricTimeline;
- LocalTempo;
- TemporalDeviation;
- MetricStability;
- InstrumentProfiles.

The PerformanceProfile provides the analytical basis for the AnalysisReport.

---

# 7. Lifecycle

The PerformanceProfile is generated after all InstrumentProfiles have been completed.

Once generated, it remains immutable.

---

# 8. Domain Invariants

The following invariants shall always remain true.

## Invariant 1

Every PerformanceProfile refers to exactly one analysed performance.

---

## Invariant 2

Every PerformanceProfile is derived exclusively from analytical results produced by the Jazz Groove Analyzer.

---

## Invariant 3

The PerformanceProfile never modifies its source analytical objects.

---

## Invariant 4

The PerformanceProfile represents analytical knowledge.

It is never directly observable.

---

## Invariant 5

The PerformanceProfile remains immutable after creation.

---

# 9. Constraints

The PerformanceProfile shall never depend upon:

- subjective musical evaluation;
- manually inserted annotations;
- external analytical systems.

Its construction depends exclusively on the analytical results generated by the Jazz Groove Analyzer.

---

# 10. Observability

The PerformanceProfile is not directly observable.

It represents the highest-level analytical synthesis of a musical performance.

# 13. Conclusion

The PerformanceProfile represents the complete analytical description of the temporal behaviour of an ensemble.

It integrates the analytical information produced throughout the Jazz Groove Analyzer pipeline into a coherent representation of the analysed musical performance.

It constitutes the principal analytical object from which reports and comparative studies are generated.
