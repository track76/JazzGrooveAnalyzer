# 1. Purpose

The purpose of the `AnalysisReport` entity is to represent the complete output generated by the Jazz Groove Analyzer following the analysis of a musical performance.

It provides an organised and reproducible representation of the analytical results without altering the underlying domain objects.

---

# 2. Scientific Motivation

The analytical process performed by the Jazz Groove Analyzer produces a large collection of interconnected results.

These results require a structured representation suitable for scientific interpretation, comparison, storage and future reproducibility.

The AnalysisReport provides this final representation.

---

# 3. Scientific Definition

An `AnalysisReport` is the final analytical entity produced by the Jazz Groove Analyzer.

It aggregates the complete set of analytical objects generated during the analysis of one musical performance.

The AnalysisReport contains references to analytical results.

It does not modify or reinterpret them.

---

---

# 5. Responsibilities

The AnalysisReport is responsible for:

- representing the final analytical output;
- collecting the complete analytical results;
- preserving traceability to the analysed performance;
- supporting export, storage and comparison of analyses.

The AnalysisReport never:

- performs signal analysis;
- modifies analytical results;
- estimates temporal structures.

---

# 6. Relationships

Each AnalysisReport refers to exactly one analysed performance.

It aggregates references to:

- PerformanceProfile;
- InstrumentProfiles;
- InternalMetricTimeline;
- LocalTempo;
- TemporalDeviation;
- MetricStability.

Future versions may include additional analytical objects.

---

# 7. Lifecycle

The AnalysisReport is generated after all analytical stages have been completed.

Once generated, it remains immutable.

---

# 8. Domain Invariants

The following invariants shall always remain true.

## Invariant 1

Every AnalysisReport refers to exactly one analysed performance.

---

## Invariant 2

The AnalysisReport never modifies analytical results.

---

## Invariant 3

Every referenced analytical object belongs to the same analysis.

---

## Invariant 4

The AnalysisReport represents analytical knowledge.

It is never directly observable.

---

## Invariant 5

The AnalysisReport remains immutable after creation.

---

# 9. Constraints

The AnalysisReport shall never depend upon:

- manually edited analytical values;
- external evaluation systems;
- subjective musical judgement.

Its content depends exclusively on the analytical results generated by the Jazz Groove Analyzer.

---

# 10. Observability

The AnalysisReport is not directly observable.

It represents the final organised synthesis of the complete analytical process.
