# F-008 — Descriptor Taxonomy

## Status

Draft

## Purpose

This document classifies Behaviour Descriptors.

## Categories

### Temporal

Descriptors measuring temporal properties.

### Persistence

Descriptors measuring persistence.

### Stability

Descriptors measuring behavioural stability.

### Density

Descriptors measuring event density.

### Regularity

Descriptors measuring regularity.

### Coordination

Descriptors measuring coordination between observable behaviours.

### Interaction

Descriptors measuring observable interactions.

## Constraint

Descriptors belonging to different categories remain independent.

No descriptor may implicitly compute another descriptor.
