of the axioms previously established.

Closure of Finite Transformations

is itself a valid Descriptor Operator.

Proof

Behaviour Space Preservation

No valid Descriptor Operator may generate
objects outside Behaviour Space.

Proof

Direct consequence of the codomain definition
of every Descriptor Operator.

Representation Preservation

No Descriptor Operator modifies
BehaviourDescriptors.

Proof

Descriptor Operators act exclusively on
Descriptor Relations.

BehaviourDescriptors belong to the
Representation Layer.

the architecture
