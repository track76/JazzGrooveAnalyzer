# F-000 — Mathematical Principles

## Status

Draft

## Purpose

This document defines the mathematical principles governing
Behaviour Analytics.

All analytical operators introduced in JGA must comply with
these principles.

---

# MP-001

Mathematics operates exclusively on validated representations.

Audio is never directly analyzed by mathematical operators.

---

# MP-002

Every mathematical operator is deterministic.

Equal inputs always produce equal outputs.

---

# MP-003

Mathematical operators are immutable.

Operators never modify their input.

They always generate a new mathematical structure.

---

# MP-004

Every mathematical structure preserves complete provenance.

Every output must remain traceable to the originating
DescriptorSet.

---

# MP-005

Mathematics never introduces musical interpretation.

Only mathematical relationships may be produced.

---

# MP-006

Every analytical transformation must explicitly declare:

Input

Transformation

Output

Preserved Invariants

---

# MP-007

No mathematical operator may access information that is not
contained in its declared input.
