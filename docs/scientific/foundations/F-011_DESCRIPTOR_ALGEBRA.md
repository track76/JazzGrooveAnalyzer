# F-011 — Descriptor Algebra

## Status

Scientific Foundation

## Purpose

This document establishes the mathematical algebra governing
Descriptor Operators.

The Descriptor Algebra defines how Descriptor Operators
can be composed while preserving Behaviour Space consistency.

## Scope

The Descriptor Algebra operates exclusively on
Descriptor Relations.

BehaviourDescriptors remain immutable.

## Mathematical Objects

Behaviour Space

↓

Descriptor Relation

↓

Descriptor Operator

↓

Descriptor Algebra

The algebra governs transformations,
not representations.

## Fundamental Principle

A Descriptor Algebra is the closed mathematical system
generated by valid Descriptor Operators acting on
Descriptor Relations.

No operation may violate the Behaviour Space.
