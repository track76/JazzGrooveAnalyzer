# BM-006 — Behaviour Continuity

## Status

Scientific Foundation

## Purpose

This document establishes the notion of Behaviour
Continuity within the Behaviour Metric Space.

Continuity formalizes the preservation of
behaviour under sufficiently small behavioural
variations.

## Scientific Principle

Observable rhythmic behaviour evolves
continuously unless an observable musical event
introduces a discontinuity.

Continuity is therefore an observable property,
not an implementation detail.

## Definition

Let

(B,d)

be the Behaviour Metric Space.

A Behaviour Functional

F : B → ℝ

is continuous at

b ∈ B

if every sufficiently small variation of behaviour
produces a sufficiently small variation of the
functional value.

## Input

Behaviour

↓

Behaviour Functional

## Transformation

Continuity Evaluation

## Output

Continuity Property

## Interpretation

Continuity describes the stability of analytical
quantities under local behavioural variation.

It does not imply behavioural identity.

It does not imply behavioural similarity.

## Properties

Behaviour Continuity

- depends on the Behaviour Metric;

- is implementation independent;

- preserves analytical consistency;

- supports local reasoning.
